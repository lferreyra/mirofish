## Source mix

This pilot is evidence-backed, but not in the same way as `MP`.

The source mix is mainly:

- official Sivers press releases
  - POET partnership
  - Ayar partnership expansion
  - WIN manufacturing collaboration
  - Q4 2025 pipeline update
  - refinancing announcement
  - LiDAR optionality context

That means:

- stronger than pure investor-post fragments
- weaker than a case dominated by government and industry-body sources

So the parse should be treated as:

- structurally useful
- commercially suggestive
- still requiring outside-source corroboration for full conviction

The key output is the `market_miss` inference:

- the market may still treat Sivers as a messy small Nordic photonics company
  even though it appears to sit upstream of multiple emerging external-light-
  source and optical-I/O architectures with improving manufacturing and

That is much closer to the actual AleaBito-style target than the earlier
pilots.

It is not just:
