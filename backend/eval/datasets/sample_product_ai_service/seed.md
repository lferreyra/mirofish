# "Cortex Writer" Consumer AI Launch

A startup launched Cortex Writer, a $15/month consumer AI writing
assistant. Professional copywriters and journalists have expressed
concerns about job displacement. Students and small-business owners have
reacted positively. Two competing AI products released similar features
at similar prices the same week. Early reviewers note the model sometimes
hallucinates citations. Social-media reception is polarized: enthusiastic
early adopters vs. vocal critics.
