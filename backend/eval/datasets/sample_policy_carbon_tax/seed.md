# Proposed National Carbon Tax

A coalition of legislators announced a phased national carbon tax targeting
large industrial emitters. The proposal includes a $40/ton starting price,
annual escalators, and revenue recycling via household rebates. Industry
groups (oil, gas, steel) have released statements opposing the measure.
Environmental NGOs and public-health groups support it. Three large
universities publish cost-benefit modeling showing long-term GDP-neutral
impact. Major newspapers have mixed editorial positions. Social-media
reaction is mixed: early engagement skews negative.
