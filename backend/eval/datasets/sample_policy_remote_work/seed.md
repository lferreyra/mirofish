# City-Wide Remote-Work Incentive Program

A mid-sized city government announced a program offering small grants to
local businesses that maintain at least 40% remote-work headcount for two
years. Goal: reduce peak commute congestion. Chamber of Commerce is
tentatively supportive. Two large downtown landlords have voiced concerns
about office-vacancy impacts. A labor union representing commuters issued
a supportive statement. Local press has run mostly neutral explainers.
