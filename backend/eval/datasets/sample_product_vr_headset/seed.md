# "Horizon Lens" VR Headset Launch

A major technology company announced Horizon Lens, a $499 VR headset
positioned at mainstream consumers. Tech reviewers have received early
units; hardware consensus is positive (wide FOV, good comfort). Software
library is thin at launch (six flagship titles, ports from older systems).
Developer support is being actively courted. Two competing headsets launch
the same quarter at different price points. Pre-order numbers have not
been published.
