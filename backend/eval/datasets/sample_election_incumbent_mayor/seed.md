# City Mayoral Election: Incumbent vs. Newcomer

A two-term incumbent mayor is running against a first-time newcomer
challenger. Incumbent has a 52% approval rating and a 10-point fundraising
lead. The local paper endorsed the incumbent. A controversial zoning
decision two years ago drew criticism. Voter-turnout in recent off-year
elections has been low. Challenger has strong online presence but limited
field organization. Three televised debates are scheduled before election
day.
